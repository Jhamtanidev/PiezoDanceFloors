Dance to Power the Future!

Dance floors that not only make you groove but also generate green electricity with every step, turning your energy into a powerful force for change.

Features

"Discover the innovative features that make our piezodance floor project an electrifying experience!"

Interactive Dance Experience

PieTech floors transforms ordinary dance floors into interactive ones.

Energy Harvesting

The eco-friendly aspect is the piezoelectric sensors harness kinetic energy from dancers' movements

Arduino Integration

The role of Arduino Uno is to serve as the brain of the system, processing sensor data and showing generated output.

Battery-Powered Operation

PieTech floors can operate wirelessly using a 6V LiPo ion battery, allowing for flexibility in installation locations without the need for external power sources.

Scalability

PieTech floors can be scaled up or down to accommodate different space requirements, making it suitable for various applications and occasions.

Future Enhancements

Potential future enhancements or iterations, such as adding Bluetooth connectivity for remote control or integrating advanced motion detection algorithms for more interactive experiences.
