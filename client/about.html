About PieTech Floors

Driven by our passion for environmental conservation, we decided to take on this design engineering project which harnesses the power of piezoelectric technology to create innovative dance floors that generate clean and renewable electricity with every dance move. We believe that the joy of dancing can power not only hearts but also the world.
